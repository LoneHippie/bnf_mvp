Welcome to the BNF survey! Throughout the survey you will be asked questions about your personal fitness history/preferences as well as some personal information regarding height, weight and injuries. While these questions may not be 100% comfortable for everyone to answer, they are necessary for us to generate a usable personalized outline for your own unique program. Any answers/data provided by users are temporary and will not be used outside of BNF and are not for sale to third-parties.
