Our system emphasizes core foundation compound movements such as a squats, push ups or pull ups. We aim to build highly functional movement patterns along with strong mind muscle connections and to develop a highly specialized and personal program for each client, the end result being a fitness program that can be completely integrated into everyday life and utilized no matter the situation or setting.

Unlike many in the industry we are not trying to sell you any magic supplements or tell you the exact percentage of rest, work, and food you need to consume. We believe sleep, proper nutrition, and the right amount of exercise are fundamental to the success and wellbeing of any athlete or client. Everyone should be able to workout indoor and out, with or without equipment, regardless of age or fitness experience. Our program is designed to be progressive in its ability to automatically increase or decrease workload over time. Your feedback, which can be set to a daily, weekly, monthly, or quarterly basis will allow us to refine your personal program to make it as effective as it can be. Most importantly we want all our Bear Necessities programs to be fun and enjoyable for everyone!

×

The process starts with our survey, in which your answers will be fed through our algorithm to tailor each program to your specific needs. Afterwards, personal touches and tweaks are made on a case to case basis by Bare Necessities Fitness staff, ready to work one on one to find the perfect routine for each and every client. Our approach to fitness is completely comprehensive, which takes as many factors into consideration as possible such as injuries, financial constraints, and fitness experience.

The fitness industry today is full of myths, false dreams, and fairytales. We hope to shed light on these issues through common sense, research, and help from people like you! The programs we have constructed follow a logical set of standards combined with tried and true movements and exercises. We have developed a system that takes much of the guesswork out of the equation, which will give you more time to focus on actually working out.

×

Our hour long programs would start off with a dynamic warm up ranging from jumping rope, a jog, bodyweight squats, or all of the above. We want the nervous system primed and the blood flowing for the main phase of the workout. After that we start from the legs and move our way up the body to the traps and shoulders. Along the way we will utilize a combination of isolation and compound movements to stimulate as many muscle fibers as possible. Depending on personal goals, our algorithm will add and subtract exercises, increase or decrease rep ranges, and or weight to meet your ever changing needs. Full range of motion, mobility, and injury prevention are concepts we believe are a few major keys to success.

Example Workout
Warm Up
  • Jump Rope 30 sec x 2
  • Jog 4 min
Isolation Exercises
  • Leg Extensions 10 x 3
  • V Sits 20 x 3
  • Cable Pulldowns 10, 8, 6
  • Hammer Curls 12, 10, 8
Compound Exercises
  • Squats 15, 10, 5
  • Wall Sits 30 sec x 2
  • Burpees 12, 10, 8
  • Plank 45 sec x 2
